MLM flag

The MLM or gay pride flag represents pride towards gay people(men who feel romantic and sexual attraction to men) the Dark green stripe represents community. The lighter green stripe represents healing. The lightest green stripe represents joy. The white stripe represents gender-non-conforming, nonbinary, and transgender men. The light blue stripe represnts pure love. The darker blue stripe represents fortitude. The darkest blue stripe represents diveristy.
